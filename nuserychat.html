About Our Nursery

Appu Nursery is a warm and welcoming environment where children can learn, play, and grow. Our team of dedicated educators provide personalized care and education to each child, helping them to reach their full potential in a safe and nurturing space.

Personalized Care

Every plant in our nursery receives individual attention and care, ensuring they are healthy and vibrant, ready to thrive in any environment..

Eco-Friendly Practices

We prioritize sustainability by using eco-friendly methods to nurture our plants. Our nursery focuses on reducing waste and promoting green initiatives. .

We offer a wide variety of plants, from indoor succulents to outdoor trees, to meet all your gardening needs. Our collection is carefully curated to suit different climates and spaces.
